Action

Multiple items to Things – with list, tags and heading

Posted by @RosemaryOrchard, Last update almost 4 years ago

This action lets you type multiple lines of text and converts each line into tasks inside Things; and also lets you specify the list and tags for the items. Example use: Multiple lines of text can be sent to the ‘Groceries’ list with the tag ‘home’. You can also specify the heading in a text field. Full credit for the original action goes to shankarux.
