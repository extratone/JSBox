TIP: Switch action groups with quick access tabs

Tips & Tricks
tip
agiletortoise May 22, 2018, 2:45pm #1

Quick access tabs add tabs across the bottom of the action list to quickly switch between your action groups. To enable tabs:

  • Tap the list options (…) button at the top right of the action list
  • Turn on quick access tabs.
  • Choose whether the tabs should display the name, icon or name & icon for each tab.

Enabling quick access tabs puts your action groups in easy reach!

Details: http://getdrafts.com/actions/action-list-options
