Drafts 2021: Year in Review

News & Updates
agiletortoise December 27, 2021, 3:18pm #1

2021 has been a busy year for Drafts. I am, generally speaking, a person who prefers to spend their time contemplating the present and future, but it’s not a bad idea to look back now and then and remind yourself of what you have accomplished.

In case you missed some of it, here are a few of the major features that have made their way into Drafts in 2021:

  • Custom Syntaxes and Themes
  • Google Task and Microsoft To Do Integration
  • Widget Theming
  • Document Scanning and Live Text Support
  • Folder Bookmarks
  • Shortcuts Support on Mac
  • New Apple Watch App
  • Auto-Complete for [[Wiki-Style]] Links
  • Global Auto-Complete Suggestions
  • Transcription of Audio and Video Files
  • Extract Text from PDFs

I would like to thank everyone who uses Drafts, those that support the app with a Pro subscription, and all the great community members who share their time and creative solutions with others – both on this forum and elsewhere!

I don’t like to be too specific about future plans, but we have a lot of good things coming in 2022…including celebrating the 10th anniversary of Drafts’ original 1.0 release (Yikes!).

Here’s hoping 2022 is great for you and yours, and thanks again for using Drafts!
