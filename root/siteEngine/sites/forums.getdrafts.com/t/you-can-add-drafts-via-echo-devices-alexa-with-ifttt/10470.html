A couple of additional points.

To use this you need to enable automatic reminders import, in Drafts, on one of your devices.

Drafts User Guide

Automatic Import

Documentation for Drafts app, a quick-capture note taking app for iPhone, iPad, Mac and Apple Watch.

You then need to ensure that the reminders list being used by the IFTTT applet also matches the reminders list you are importing from. i.e. if you use reminders for non-Drafts importing then you want to specify a particular reminders list. It will still pull in anything you add to your Echo‚Äôs todo list, but then you are keeping it separate from your other reminders - particularly your default.
