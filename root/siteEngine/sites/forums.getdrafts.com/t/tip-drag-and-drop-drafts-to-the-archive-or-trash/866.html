TIP: Drag and drop drafts to the archive or trash

Tips & Tricks
drag-and-drop, tip
agiletortoise May 8, 2018, 2:27pm #1

The inbox, flagged, archive and trash tabs in the draft list support drag and drop. Dropping drafts on the inbox, archive or trash will move the selected drafts to that folder. Dropping on the flagged tab will assign flagged status.
