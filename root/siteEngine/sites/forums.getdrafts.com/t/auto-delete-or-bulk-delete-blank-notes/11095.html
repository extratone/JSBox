You can search for them by using regular expressions. A search /^.{0}$/ will find all blank drafts, you than switch to the ‚ÄúAll‚Äù tab, select-all (tap and hold on ‚ÄúSelect‚Äù on iOS for options) > Operations > Trash.

// loop over and delete them

  d.isTrashed = true;

Curious, however, how you are ending up with a lot of blank drafts.
