I work for a company selling hiking holidays, and have to hike and describe the trails before customers are sold a holiday.

During a hike I will need to periodically stop to describe a turn or a distinctive feature, and need to capture the time and location of such stops. Doing this manually with pen and paper is laborious. Drafts is a perfect solution, and a network signal is not required.

This walk writing workflow allows me to record to record date/time, location and comments (dictated) in Drafts, and tags the notes I take.
