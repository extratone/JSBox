Add or remove friends in Find My on Mac

After you share your location with friends, you can ask to follow their locations or remove them if you no longer want to see their locations.

Open Find My for me

Ask to see a friends location

When you ask to see a friends location, your friend receives a notification with your request. After your friend accepts your request, you can see their location.

  1. In the Find My app on your Mac, click People.

  2. In the People list, select a name, then click the Info button on the map.

  3. Click Ask To Follow Location.

    Tip: To add your friend as a favorite, click the Info button on the map, then click Add [Friend] to Favorites.

If you see your friends name in the People list but cant see their location on the map, ask them to make sure theyre sharing their location and that they turned on Location Services on their device.

Remove a friend

When you remove a friend, that person is removed from your People list and youre removed from theirs. That person doesnt receive a notification that you removed them.

  1. In the Find My app on your Mac, click People.

  2. In the People list, select a name, then click the Info button on the map.

  3. Click Remove [Friend] or Stop Sharing My Location.

    Note: You can also Control-click a name in the People list, then choose Remove [Friend].
