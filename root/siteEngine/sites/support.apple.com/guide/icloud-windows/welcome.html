Use iCloud on your PC

iCloud for Windows lets you access your iCloud photos, files, email, passwords, and more on your Windows computer.

Learn what features are available

To explore the iCloud for Windows User Guide, click Table of Contents at the top of the page, or enter a word or phrase in the search field.
