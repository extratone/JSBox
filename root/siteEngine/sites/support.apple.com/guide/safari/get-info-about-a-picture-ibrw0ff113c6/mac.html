Get information about a picture in Safari on Mac

Look up a brief summary about whats in a photofor example, a work of art, a historical landmark, plants and flowers, books, a breed of pet, and more.

  1. In the Safari app on your Mac, go to a photo or image.

  2. Control-click the image, then choose Look Up.
