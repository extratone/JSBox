Create your Memoji in Messages on Mac

With macOS 11 or later, you can create a personalized Memoji that matches your personality. Then, send Memoji stickers to express your mood in your messages.

Open Messages for me

  1. In the Messages app on your Mac, select any conversation.

  2. To the left of the field at the bottom of the window, click the Apps button , then select the Memoji Stickers button .

  3. Click the Add button , then follow the onscreen instructions to customize your Memoji, starting with skin tone all the way through clothing.

  4. Click Done.

You can also use your Memoji on the login window.
