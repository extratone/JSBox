Todoist Smart Keyboard

This is used in tandem with the Todoist Quick Add V3 action. It’s shown as both Available in List and Available in Action Bar, but I mainly use it as a keyboard when entering multiple tasks at once on iOS.

This gives me an easy way to keep up with clients’ billing hours to match against our system at month’s end.
